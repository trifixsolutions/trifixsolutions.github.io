# Palette UX & Accessibility Journal

## 2026-02-12 - Enhancing Form Feedback and Semantics
**Learning:** Replacing generic `alert()` calls with in-page feedback and micro-UX states (like "Sending...") significantly improves the perceived quality and accessibility of form interactions.
**Action:** Use `#form-status` with `aria-live="polite"` and button loading states for all future form implementations.
